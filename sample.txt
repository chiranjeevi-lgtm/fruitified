Shelf Life and Storage
Royal Gala apples have a shelf life of 5 days and are best consumed as soon as they arrive. If you plan to eat them within a day or two, you can keep them on the kitchen countertop. However, if you don't plan to consume them that quickly, it's better to place them in a plastic bag and store them in the refrigerator, as this will keep them fresher for longer. Additionally, avoid storing them with other fruits because apples produce ethylene gas, which can accelerate the ripening and spoilage of nearby fruits and vegetables.

High in Potassium:
Apples are high in potassium, a key mineral needed to prevent high blood pressure by removing sodium from the blood. Moreover, it helps maintain electrolyte and fluid balance within the muscle and nerve cells.
